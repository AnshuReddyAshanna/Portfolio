About Me

Hi! I’m Anshu Reddy, a developer who works at the interface of data, intelligence, and design, where curiosity meets structure. Most of my time goes into building AI and machine learning models, exploring datasets to uncover meaningful patterns, and creating dashboards that turn complex information into something intuitive and usable. I enjoy working with real-world data, training models, and shaping analytics tools that don’t just function, but communicate clearly.

Through my projects, I’ve learned how to think critically about data, evaluate models with care, and translate raw numbers into insights that actually matter. I also enjoy experimenting with Generative AI tools and large language models, spending time understanding how these systems work under the hood and how they can be applied responsibly and creatively.What I enjoy most is the process itself, asking the right questions, refining models, iterating on ideas, and designing outputs that feel clean and intentional. I have a strong interest in UI design as well, because I believe intelligent systems should be as clear and approachable as they are powerful.

Looking ahead, I want to continue building scalable, intelligent systems that learn from data and evolve with real-world use. I’m driven by curiosity, grounded in problem-solving, and always looking for ways to make things work a little better than they did before.
