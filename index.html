About Me

Hi! I’m Anshu Reddy Ashanna, a developer who works across AI/ML, data science, and visualizations. I enjoy the full lifecycle of building data-driven systems: preprocessing data, training models, validating results, and delivering the output into something clear like a dashboard, a report, or a usable interface.

I’ve built machine learning models for classification and prediction, explored statistical patterns in public health datasets, and designed interactive dashboards that use APIs for real-time insights. I also spend time experimenting with generative AI tools and LLMs, not just to “use” them, but to understand what makes them reliable, where they fail, and how to keep outputs grounded. Clarity is what matters most to me: robust evaluation, organized structure, and evidence-based decisions. I have a strong interest in UI/UX as well, because I believe intelligent systems should be clear and approachable as they are powerful.

Going forward, I want to continue building scalable, intelligent systems that learn from data and evolve with real-world use. I’m looking to work where I can contribute across modeling, analysis, and product-based delivery, and where the goal is to make things work a little better than they did before.
